About Andrea

Hi, I’m Andrea, and I work at the intersection of e-learning, reporting, and communication, with a strong international and EU-focused background. I hold a Master’s degree in International Economics and Business Management from the Università degli Studi di Verona, where my final thesis analysed the economic integration of ASEAN countries, strengthening my understanding of international cooperation, trade dynamics, and policy frameworks.

My academic and professional path has been consistently international. I spent an Erasmus period in Romania and gained professional experience across several European countries. In Poland, I worked as an International Marketing Specialist, where I developed LinkedIn campaigns, created visual and written content, and adapted communication strategies to different target audiences. I also worked in the tourism sector for internationally oriented companies such as Girolibero, contributing to multilingual promotional materials, translations, and operational coordination.

I am currently working at the European Labour Authority (ELA), where I support capacity-building activities through LMS administration, e-learning coordination, and data reporting. My responsibilities include managing Moodle platforms, uploading and testing training content, creating custom reports, and exporting data to Power BI dashboards to monitor user engagement, attendance, and learning outcomes. Having worked in EU institutions, I’m particularly interested in international relations and geopolitics, and in how historical and economic dynamics shape policy decisions.

Previously, I worked in the banking software sector as a Quality Assurance and Database Specialist, performing functional testing and working with SQL databases and Excel-based reporting. This experience strengthened my analytical skills, structured thinking, and attention to detail, which I now apply to reporting, data-driven communication, and training evaluation.

To complement my professional experience, I completed several certifications, including the IBM Data Analyst Professional Certificate and the Google Digital Marketing & E-commerce Certificate, gaining practical experience with SQL, Power BI, analytics, SEO, email marketing, and performance measurement. I have also passed the EPSO/CAST/P/13/2017 competition for Political Affairs / EU Policies, confirming my familiarity with EU institutional frameworks and policy-oriented environments.
