Pension Case Study: EU Rules in action

This presentation provides a detailed case study on how pensions are calculated for individuals who have worked across multiple EU borders. It highlights the professional journey of Alina, who accumulated a total of 40 years of work experience across Romania, France, and Hungary. The materials break down the EU's pro-rata calculation method, explaining how each member state pays a proportional share of the pension based on the specific years worked within its borders. Additionally, it details how these partial payments combine to form a full retirement entitlement. The final presentation demonstrates how these regulations ensure fairness and prevent benefit gaps or overlaps, guaranteeing that workers receive exactly what they have earned.

Fram.com: Graphic Design Project

This project involves designing a flyer and brochure for a hotel event hosted by Fram.com. The materials should align with the upscale and modern identity of Fram Hotel, a boutique establishment known for its sophisticated yet inviting ambiance. The brochure will highlight the hotel's luxurious rooms, premium facilities, and high-quality dining options. Additionally, it will feature details about the event, including a 6:00 PM reception with supper, messages to the bride and groom, cocktails, and dancing. The final design should reflect the elegance and exclusivity of the venue while ensuring clear and engaging communication.
